About Insulin Buddy

What is it for?

Insulin Buddy is a website for helping you manage your diabetes. It provides a friendly, motivating environment in which to record your daily insulin doses taken, blood glucose, weight, and exercise measurements. This information is directly linked to your clinician, allowing them to monitor your health status and send you encouraging messages.

About the creators

We're a team of university students studying web development at the University of Melbourne. We love creating websites, and we love helping other people, so we were excited to do both with Insulin Buddy.
